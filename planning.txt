                        DATASET info
Dataset name: GTZAN (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/andradaolteanu/gtzan-dataset-music-genre-classification/data)
Folder descriptions:
    genres_original - a collections of 10 genres with 100 audio files each, all having 30 seconds.
    images_original - a visual representation for each audio file. Audio files are converted to Mel Spectrograms
    2 CSV files     - containing features of the audio files that can be extracted from the audio files.
